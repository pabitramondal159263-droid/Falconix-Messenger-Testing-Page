Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Our Zero-Knowledge Commitment

At Falconix Messenger, your privacy is our foundational principle. We operate on a strict zero-knowledge architecture. This means we cannot read, access, or decrypt any of your messages, voice calls, video calls, or shared files. Your cryptographic keys are generated and stored entirely on your local device (via IndexedDB), ensuring absolute confidentiality.

2. Information We Collect

To provide our services, we collect the absolute minimum data required:

  • Account Information: Your email address (used solely for authentication) and an optional profile name/bio.
  • Public Keys: We store your RSA-2048 public key to allow other users to encrypt messages intended for you. We never possess your private key.
  • Encrypted Data: We temporarily route AES-256-GCM encrypted message blobs and files. We have no mathematical way to decrypt this data.

3. End-to-End Encryption (E2EE)

Every message, file, and call made through Falconix Messenger is secured using military-grade encryption:

  • Messages & Files: Encrypted using AES-256-GCM. The AES key is then encrypted with the recipient's RSA-2048 Public Key.
  • Calls: Handled via WebRTC with enforced DTLS-SRTP encryption.
  • Forward Secrecy: Temporary keys are generated for sessions to ensure maximum forward secrecy.

4. Disappearing Messages

If you utilize our Disappearing Messages feature, messages are programmed to self-destruct from both local devices and our routing servers after the designated timeframe (e.g., 1 hour, 24 hours). Once deleted, they cannot be recovered by any party.

5. Third-Party Services

We do not sell, rent, or monetize your data. We utilize secure backend infrastructure (like Firebase) strictly for the routing of encrypted packets and authentication. These providers adhere to stringent security standards and cannot bypass the end-to-end encryption protecting your content.

6. Your Rights & Data Deletion

You have full control over your digital identity. Since we do not hold your private keys, losing your device or failing to export your Identity Backup means your past encrypted data becomes permanently inaccessible. You can delete your account and purge your public data from our servers at any time through the app settings.

7. Changes to This Policy

As we enhance our cryptographic protocols and app features, we may update this Privacy Policy. We will notify users of significant changes through the app. Continued use of Falconix Messenger implies acceptance of the updated policy.
